Smart Product Blocks That Convert — Anywhere

Kota helps creators, bloggers, and e-commerce sellers combine their Shopify products with Amazon and Walmart affiliate offers — into powerful AI-powered product blocks that work across your blog, store, YouTube, and email.

Core Features

Shopify + Affiliate Integration

Combine owned Shopify products with Amazon and Walmart affiliate offers in unified blocks.

Amazon + Walmart Product Sync

AI Copy & Recommendations

Universal Embed System

Embed anywhere: WordPress, Shopify, Ghost, Webflow, Substack, email, YouTube overlays.

Analytics Dashboard
